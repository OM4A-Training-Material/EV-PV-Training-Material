This folder contains the materials used for the hands-on training session on EVPV held on 6 May in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, as part of the broader e-mobility workshop (6–8 May).

The training session focused on the practical use of EVPV within the GIS Tool, with an emphasis on spatial analysis and e-mobility planning applications.
